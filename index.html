Welcome to the Dictionary Game!

In this game, you will be given a starting word and a target word. Your goal is to use the words in the definition or in the related words to get to the target.

Each word in the definition is hyperlinked to its definition. You will also find related words on the page; these are also hyperlinked. How long will it take you to reach your target? Click the 'Game' tab to get started!
